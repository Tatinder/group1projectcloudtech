Our Cloud Services

At CloudTech, we offer a variety of cloud services to meet your business needs. Explore our offerings below:

Infrastructure as a Service (IaaS)

IaaS provides virtualized computing resources over the internet. It allows businesses to rent infrastructure like servers, storage, and networks, reducing the need for physical hardware. IaaS is ideal for companies that need scalability and flexibility without the burden of managing physical resources.

Platform as a Service (PaaS)

PaaS offers a platform allowing users to develop, run, and manage applications without dealing with the underlying infrastructure. It simplifies application development by providing a managed environment that supports development and deployment of applications, enabling teams to focus on coding and innovation.

Software as a Service (SaaS)

SaaS delivers software applications over the internet on a subscription basis. Users can access applications through a web browser without needing to install or maintain software, making it convenient for end-users and reducing costs for companies in terms of maintenance and deployment.
